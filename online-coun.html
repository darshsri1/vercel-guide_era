Counselling MCQs

1. What are your post-12th educational goals?

2. What field of study or career path interests you the most at this point?

3. How do you prefer to learn and acquire knowledge?

4. What is your preferred mode of further education?

5. What factors are most important to you when choosing a college or university?

6. Do you have any specific career goals in mind at this stage?

7. How do you plan to finance your higher education?

8. Have you considered any alternative education paths, such as apprenticeships or trade schools?

9. Are there any specific challenges or concerns you have regarding your post-12th education and career choices?

10. How do you envision your career five years from now?
